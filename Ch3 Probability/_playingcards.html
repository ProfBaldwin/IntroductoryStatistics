There are 52 cards in a deck of playing cards.

A deck is divided into 4 suits: Spades (♠), Hearts (♥), Diamonds (♦), and Clubs (♣).

Spades (♠) and Clubs (♣) are black. Hearts (♥) and Diamonds (♦) are red.

There are 13 ranks in each suit:
Ace, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, Jack, Queen, and King.

The Jacks, Queens, and Kings in a deck are called face cards.
